Actor since 2014

Lex Mayson is a Vancouver based, biracial, trans non-binary actor who has been studying acting for 10+ years. They play Saff in the Peacock adaptation of Tiger King, Joe vs Carole, and have appeared in other titles such as Batwoman, Motherland, and Alert: Missing Persons Unit. Their interests include cosplay, playing piano, and petting their cats, and they are excited to bring more diversity to the big screen.
